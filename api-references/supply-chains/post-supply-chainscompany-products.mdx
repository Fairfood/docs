sidebarTitle:  Add Company Product
description: The Add Company Product endpoint enables users to add a new or existing product associated with a company within a supply chain. This is achieved by sending a POST request to the specified URL, providing essential information such as product details, company affiliation, and optional forms and premiums. The response includes the newly created product's details, confirming its addition to the supply chain.







openapi: post /supply-chains/company-products/
