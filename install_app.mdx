To install the Fairfood Collector app from the Google Play Store, follow these steps:
1. Open the Google Play Store on your device.
2. In the search bar, type "Fairfood" and press enter
3. Look for the Fairfood Collector app and click on it.
4. On the app's page, click the "Install" button.

Alternatively, you can also click on this link to be taken directly to the app's page on the Google Play Store

    To update the Fairfood Collector app from the Google Play Store, follow these steps:
1. Open the Google Play Store on your device.
2. In the search bar, type "Fairfood" and press enter.
3. Look for the Fairfood Collector app and click on it.
4. On the app's page, click the "Update" button. If the "Update" button is not present, it means that the app is already up to date.
