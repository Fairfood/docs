    To update the Fairfood Collector app from the Google Play Store, follow these steps:
1. You need to connect to the internet to log in.
2. Enter your email and password and click the login button.
3. Once your credentials are validated in, you will be redirected to the synching screen.
4. We will sync all the data from the server and it can take from 1 to 10 minutes
5. Once the synching is completed You can click on next to go to your home screen.

**Note:** _You need to stay connected to the internet in the entire synching process. If the connection get disconnected then you need to re-start the process by killing the app._
