    - When you click the **Buy** button, the app will ask if it can use your device's location.  
    - You need to choose the option that says **"Allow while using the app."**  
    - After you give permission, the app won’t ask for your location again next time you use it.  
    - After clicking **Buy**, you will go to the **Choose Product** page.

     <img
  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/app-img/chooseproduct.png"
  alt="connection"
/>
<img
  className="hidden dark:block"
  src="/images/app-img/chooseproduct-b.png"

   - When you click the **Buy** option, you will be taken to the **Choose Product** page.  
- On this page, you can select one of the available products to purchase.


**Note:**  _Products are mapped against the supply chain. Based on the supply chains this product will list on Choose product page._

  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Quantity and Base price">
   <img

  className="hidden dark:block"

     - Once you choose a product, you will go to the next page.  
     - On this page, you can type in how many items you want to buy (quantity) and the price of each item (base price).
  </Step>

 - On the same page, there will be an **Add another product** option to add more products.  
- When you click **Add another product**, it will show a list of all products along with their status.

**Note:**  _We can not add another product without adding the Quantity and base price for the selected products._
  </Step>

   - On the **Premium Verification** page, you can scan either an **NFC card** or a **QR code** to share data and transactions for traceability and storytelling.  
   - There is also a **text-to-voice** feature that announces the transaction details. To enable this, you need to turn it on in the **settings**.  
   - If you don’t have an NFC card or QR code, you can select the **No card** option, where you can manually add a farmer and update the details.
